What is My Kitchen Pantry?

My Kitchen Pantry is a home kitchen organizer that helps you keep track of what you have, plan meals around your pantry, create shopping lists, and stay more aware of expiration dates and allergy needs.

See the app in action

A quick look at the main screens that help you organize food, plan meals, and shop with less guesswork.

Start from your kitchen dashboard

See your pantry, meals, shopping, recipes, allergies, and account tools from one simple home screen.

Track what you have

Keep up with pantry items, quantities, categories, and expiration dates.

Plan meals with awareness

Organize meals for the week and, with Pro, see allergy warnings while planning.

Build shopping lists

Add items, adjust quantities, share your list, and keep grocery planning organized.

What it helps you do

Track your pantry

Keep up with pantry items, quantities, categories, and expiration dates so you know what you already have.

Plan meals

Add meals to your weekly plan, organize dinners ahead of time, and keep your kitchen routine easier to manage.

Build shopping lists

Create shopping lists, adjust quantities, share your list, and keep track of what still needs to be picked up.

Use recipe ideas

Browse recipes, view ingredients, and use pantry-aware tools to help turn what you have into meal ideas.

Reduce food waste

Expiration labels and pantry visibility help you use food before it goes bad and avoid buying duplicates.

Allergy awareness with Pro

Pro unlocks allergy tracking and allergy warnings across recipes and meal planning tools.
