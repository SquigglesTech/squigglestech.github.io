Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how My Kitchen Pantry, published by SquigglesTech LLC, collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the app.

1. Who we are

My Kitchen Pantry is a pantry, meal planning, shopping list, recipe, and household food organization app.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at support@squigglestech.com.

2. Information we collect

Depending on how you use the app, we may collect, store, or process the following information:

  • Account information, such as your email address, if you create an account or sign in.
  • Pantry, shopping list, meal plan, recipe, allergy, and related household data that you enter into the app.
  • Product lookup or barcode-related information when you use barcode scanning features.
  • Device or app information needed for app functionality, security, syncing, troubleshooting, and subscription features.
  • Purchase and subscription status information from Google Play for paid features.

3. Camera permission

My Kitchen Pantry may request access to your device camera in order to scan barcodes and help identify food or pantry items. We do not use the camera for unrelated purposes.

4. How we use information

We use information to:

  • Provide and operate the app’s pantry, recipes, shopping, meal planning, and allergy-related features.
  • Save your data locally on your device and, if you sign in, sync eligible data across your devices.
  • Authenticate your account and help secure your access.
  • Provide customer support and respond to questions.
  • Process and manage subscriptions and paid features.
  • Maintain, improve, and troubleshoot app performance and reliability.

5. Local storage and cloud sync

Some data may be stored locally on your device for app functionality. If you choose to sign in and use sync-enabled features, certain data may also be stored in cloud services used by the app so it can be available across devices.

6. Third-party services

My Kitchen Pantry may use third-party services to operate app features, including services for:

  • Authentication and cloud syncing
  • Subscription and billing management through Google Play
  • Food or product lookup and barcode-related features

Third-party services may process information as needed to provide their services in accordance with their own policies and agreements.

7. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information only as needed to:

  • Provide app functionality through service providers and platform partners
  • Process purchases and subscriptions
  • Comply with legal obligations, enforce our terms, or protect users, our business, or the public

8. Data retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the app, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Data stored locally may remain on your device until you remove the app or delete the data. Synced account data may remain in connected services until deleted by you or by us in response to a valid request.

9. Account deletion and data deletion

If you create an account, you may have the ability to delete your account and/or delete household or synced data from within the app, depending on the feature available in your version of the app.

You may also contact support@squigglestech.com for help with account or data deletion requests.

10. Children’s privacy

My Kitchen Pantry is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can review and address the issue.

11. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information, but no method of storage or transmission is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

12. Your choices

  • You can choose whether to create an account.
  • You can choose whether to use sync-enabled features.
  • You can choose whether to grant camera permission for barcode scanning.
  • You can request support regarding your account or stored data.

13. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we may update the effective date above and, where appropriate, provide additional notice.
