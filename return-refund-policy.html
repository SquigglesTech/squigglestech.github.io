Return & Refund Policy

Effective Date: February 2026

This Return and Refund Policy explains how refunds and cancellations are handled for purchases made through the My Kitchen Pantry (Pro) mobile application, published by SquigglesTech.

Payments and Processing

All in‑app purchases and subscriptions are processed securely through the Apple App Store and Google Play Billing systems via RevenueCat. SquigglesTech does not collect or store any payment information.

Refund Requests

Because all payments are handled by Apple and Google, refund requests must be made directly to the store where the purchase was completed in accordance with their respective refund policies:

  • Apple App Store: reportaproblem.apple.com
  • Google Play Store: play.google.com → Order History → Request a Refund

Free Trial

A 7‑day free trial is available for new users. At the end of the trial period, users are not automatically charged. The app reverts to the free version, and premium features are locked until users choose to upgrade to My Kitchen Pantry Pro.

Automatic Renewals

If you manually choose a paid subscription plan, it will renew automatically based on the interval selected (monthly or annual) unless you cancel through your Apple App Store or Google Play account settings before the renewal date.
