Return & Refund Policy

Effective Date: February 2026

This Return and Refund Policy explains how purchases, free trials, and refunds are handled for the My Kitchen Pantry (Pro) mobile application, developed and maintained by SquigglesTech.

Payments and Processing

All in‑app purchases and subscriptions are processed securely through the Apple App Store and Google Play Billing systems via RevenueCat. SquigglesTech does not collect or store any user payment information.

Refund Requests

Because all transactions are handled by Apple and Google, refund requests must be made directly through the store where the purchase was made. Please refer to their official refund pages for assistance:

  • Apple App Store: reportaproblem.apple.com
  • Google Play Store: play.google.com → Order History → Request a Refund

Free Trial Policy

A 7‑day free trial is available for new users. At the end of the trial period, users are not automatically charged. The app reverts to the free version, and premium features remain locked until the user chooses to upgrade to My Kitchen Pantry Pro through their app store.

Paid Subscriptions and Renewals

When a user manually selects a paid subscription plan, it renews automatically based on the chosen interval (monthly or annual) unless canceled through their Apple App Store or Google Play account settings before the next billing date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Return and Refund Policy, please contact SquigglesTech at support@squigglestech.com.
