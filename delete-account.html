Delete Your My Kitchen Pantry Account or Data

Effective date: May 2026

My Kitchen Pantry, published by SquigglesTech LLC, gives users ways to request deletion of their account and/or app data.

Option 1: Delete your account in the app

If you have access to the app, you can request account deletion directly from within My Kitchen Pantry using the account/settings area.

Deleting your app account deletes the account and associated synced user data, subject to any limited retention described below.

Option 2: Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, or if you want help, contact us at support@squigglestech.com.

Please include the email address associated with your My Kitchen Pantry account and clearly state whether you want:

  • Account deletion, or
  • Data deletion without deleting the account.

Data deletion without deleting your account

If you want to keep your app account but request deletion of certain synced data, you may request deletion of eligible app data without deleting the account.

This may include, as applicable:

  • Synced pantry data
  • Synced shopping list data
  • Synced meal planning data
  • Synced recipe-related data
  • Synced allergy or household-related data associated with the account

Depending on the request, some account-related information may remain so the account can continue to function.

What is deleted if you request full account deletion

When your account deletion request is completed, we will delete your My Kitchen Pantry app account and the user data associated with that account, subject to any limited retention described below.

This may include, as applicable:

  • Account information used for sign-in
  • Synced pantry data
  • Synced shopping list data
  • Synced meal planning data
  • Synced recipe-related data
  • Synced allergy or household-related data associated with the account

What may be retained

Certain limited information may be retained where necessary for legitimate business, security, fraud-prevention, legal, or compliance reasons. Any retained data will be kept only as long as reasonably necessary for those purposes.

Processing time

We will process deletion requests within a reasonable timeframe after verifying the request.
