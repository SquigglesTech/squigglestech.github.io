Delete Your My Kitchen Pantry Account or App Data

Effective date: May 2026

My Kitchen Pantry, published by SquigglesTech LLC, gives users a way to request deletion of their account and associated data, or to request deletion of app data without deleting their account.

Option 1: Delete your account in the app

If you have access to the app, you can request full account deletion directly from within My Kitchen Pantry using the account/settings area.

Full account deletion deletes your My Kitchen Pantry account and the user data associated with that account, subject to the limited retention described below.

Option 2: Delete household/app data without deleting your account

If you want to keep your account but delete your household/app data, you can use the Delete Household Data option in the app.

This allows you to delete synced household/app data without deleting your sign-in account.

Option 3: Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, or if you need help deleting your account or app data, you can request deletion by contacting us at support@squigglestech.com.

Please include the email address associated with your My Kitchen Pantry account and tell us whether you are requesting:

  • full account deletion, or
  • deletion of household/app data without deleting your account.

What is deleted

Depending on the request, deletion may include:

  • Account information used for sign-in
  • Synced pantry data
  • Synced shopping list data
  • Synced meal planning data
  • Synced recipe-related data
  • Synced allergy or household-related data associated with the account

If you request full account deletion, your My Kitchen Pantry account and associated app data will be deleted.

If you request household/app data deletion without deleting your account, your synced household/app data will be deleted, but your sign-in account may remain active.

What may be retained

Certain limited information may be retained where necessary for legitimate business, security, fraud-prevention, legal, or compliance reasons. Any retained data will be kept only as long as reasonably necessary for those purposes.

Processing time

We will process deletion requests within a reasonable timeframe after verifying the request.
